  • Kivish
    Kivish is a high-tech state in Siberia with a single government over three territories (Kivish, Northern Kivish, and African Kivish), known for its advanced industry, alternative history, and a strict yet pragmatic foreign policy.
  • Tarifa
    Tarifa is a private Kivish engineering company founded in 1976 in City D. It is known for a pragmatic approach: from airfield tow tractors and runway service equipment to export vans, tractors, and special-purpose machinery. In the 2000s–2010s, Tarifa became one of the symbols of “no-nonsense” transport—simple, durable, and straightforward—and some of the company’s design choices gained cult status thanks to an unusually long model life cycle.
