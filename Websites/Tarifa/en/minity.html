History of the Minity model

Tarifa Minity appeared in 1994 as a passenger model designed for families, service trips, and the everyday transport of several people in one body style. For Tarifa, this was a separate step toward a civilian minivan rather than a continuation of the lightweight passenger-car line.

The first version of Minity quickly became established as a spacious and practical vehicle focused on interior room, comfortable seating, and cabin versatility. Over time, the model stopped being seen as just utilitarian passenger transport and began to be treated as a full independent minivan with its own role in the lineup.

In 2005, Minity received a major design rethink, and in 2022 the model went through a full modern update. In its current form, it has taken the place of a large family-and-service MPV for long trips, transfers, and calm everyday operation.
