History of Tarifa airport equipment

Airport equipment was Tarifa’s original line of business from the moment the company was founded in 1976. The brand began with apron vehicles, towing machines, and service equipment long before it entered the market for commercial vans and civilian machinery.

The first generations were utilitarian and extremely simple, but they quickly became known for durability and the ability to work in harsh conditions without complicated maintenance. Later, the line received more powerful drivetrains, enclosed cabins, and new versions for different airport tasks.

The modern Airport Tug line no longer looks like bare-bones service equipment: it has become a distinct Tarifa vehicle class that combines towing capability, operator safety, service-focused design, and adaptation for continuous airfield operation.
