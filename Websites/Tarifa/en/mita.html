History of the Mita model

Tarifa Mita appeared in 1987, when the company entered the construction and agricultural equipment segments. That was when Tarifa introduced the Mita excavator as a separate heavy machine rather than part of the van line.

By the 1990s, Mita had already become an established working line: the company promoted early Mita versions in external markets alongside HK, positioning the model as heavy equipment for construction and infrastructure work. In 2000, during Tarifa’s name reorganization, the Mita line kept its own name.

The next major stage came in 2022, when Tarifa thoroughly modernized the Mita’s exterior and ergonomics, bringing the model closer to its current form.
