History of the Misi-N model

Tarifa Misi-N first appeared in 2011 as the first mass-market passenger model of the Tarifa Auto division. It was launched for Kivish Korea as a modest, inexpensive, and non-intimidating car for beginners, created to quickly give the market the most affordable form of personal transport possible.

The first version was built around extremely low ownership costs, simple construction, and easy repairability. That is exactly why Misi-N became established as a basic city car for short trips, driver training, and everyday use.

In 2023, production of the first version was fully discontinued, and in 2025 Misi-N returned in a reworked form. The new version kept the model’s ultra-compact idea, but received a cleaner presentation, an updated body, and a more modern urban role.
