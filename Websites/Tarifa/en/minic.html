History of the Minic model

Tarifa Minic appeared in 2026 as a completely new passenger-car model from the Tarifa Auto division. The car was designed from the start as a compact city hatchback for everyday use, without a tilt toward commercial format or heavy-duty versatility.

The main idea behind Minic was to create a new basic model for daily driving: compact on the outside, easy to use in the city, with a proper seating position, a modern interior, and a calm design without visual overload.

As a result, Minic took the place of Tarifa Auto’s new mass-market model — a five-door hatchback for ordinary personal use, urban environments, and everyday life.
