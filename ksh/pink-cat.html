Рози кош-лин

Рози кош-лин — редк дом-коши line of Кiвiшь, marked by natural pink-fur body. First live-read came 2016 year; full earth-adapt got public-confirm en 2026 after long bio-study around height-air need, growth-shift i ground-survive.

Вид-начал

First pink коша got found en 2016 year inside Ногара. Fur-color looked impossible at first read, but bio-gene teams of Kivish confirmed no paint, no illness-mask, no outer stain. Animal received the name Lucy and fast turned into study-core for whole line. Media-wave inside Kivish read Lucy as sign of rare bio-turn, not circus oddity.

Перш-котя loss

Early kitten-gens died without clear outer wound. For long slot teams saw stable birth and then sudden fade. Break-point came after one Japan-girl took newborn upward in aircraft-cabin; there the kitten unexpectedly revived. From that epizod labs locked main clue: first-stage bodies breathe easier en thinned air-layer. Ground-density, not visible trauma, had been killing the earliest young.

Lucy project i Джапья-совмест

Кiвiшь and Джапья launched joint study-chain under name Lucy. Teams compared blood-gas, bone-growth, fur-line and lung-form through staged pressure rooms. Result-model showed a double phase: kitten-body needed sparse air in early growth, but later adult body could slowly re-tune and hold ordinary ground-atmos. This was the key pivot that moved the line from mystery-loss toward stable breeding.

Новы лица i лин-поды

En 2024 three later animals entered the register: Lucy, Maki and Gitl. Study of fur-body split the line into two stable faces:

  • Рози Пух — long-fur body, soft layer, pearl-pink gleam.
  • Рози Интелигент — short-fur body, smooth coat, clearer contour.

Though both stay one broad line, public image quickly fixed these two faces as separate sub-lines. Breeders, labs and media all started using the paired names.

Публик-яв 2026

After a quiet year, 2026 brought the public reveal: live pink cats stood calm on ordinary ground with no pressure-assist. Official voice from Kivish stated the line had crossed the danger-gap: adult forms now adapt to any normal height-band. Two animals later went to Джапья — female Chiki of the Рози Пух line and male Kioto of the Рози Интелигент line. Their transfer became a soft symbol of trust and science-link between the two states.

Мир-реакт

World response split by region but stayed huge almost everywhere. USA and Europe moved into hype, argument and bio-ethics talk. Джапья responded with direct affection: city events, art-lines and soft festival culture formed around the new cats. After rough diplom-slots of the 2020s, the pink line began reading as a peace-symbol more than a lab-object. In Kivish itself the line became both science-pride and pop-sonda.

Лин-лик i страно-сонда

Рози кош-лин now stands at crossing of bio-study, culture-sign and diplomacy-soft power. For Kivish it proved that rare mutation could be studied without panic-kill or quick spectacle. For external world it turned into one of the strangest and warmest Kivish exports of the decade.
