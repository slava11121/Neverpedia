Ноч Флама

Ноч Флама (engl. Night of Flame Incident) — мас-терор диверс-ход en Кiвiшь Корина Эрjноти. 31 октябр — 16 ноябр 2041 годэ: синхрон-фир, дым-вал, гидролом i электро-срез. Жертв-мас дошёл до ~28 миллiон. Регiон later lost стар-нэр i ушёл en Эко Кiвiшь.

Старт-ночь came 31 октябр / 1 ноябр shift, when сотни фир-очаг lit en жил-квадры, индуст-узлы i путiно-инфра. Few часы later whole зема got зарево-halo, комм-сети fell, воздух thickened with дым i tox-gas. След-ход later named operation as long-plan grid: mini-группы 4–6 лиц came en зема under турист, транзит i сезон-work skins, then stashed фракс-гел, timер-klad, dry-smoke tools i water-grid sabots around city-core.

Подгот-ход

  • Заброс mini-ячей по 4–6 лиц under travel-work skins.
  • Scout of weak-узлы: чердак-pass, вент-shafts, мусор-tubes, тех-под, gas-line, heat-line, power-split, fuel-yards i рынк-склады.
  • Stash of fire-tools: флакс-гел, therm-skald mix, tимер-locks, micro-burst pushers for огнь-разнос, hydr-block i pump-jam inserts.
  • Mask-laya: twin-docs, clean-link devices, prep-account shells, shortwave hush-lines.

Ход-волны

Перва волна (23:40–00:15)

First фир-lines rose en жил-массы i индуст-zones. Vertical city-body turned into огнь-lift: вентиля-шафты, мусор-drop, lift-pits i dry-chim stems pulled фир upward. En power-shields, alum-therm inserts burst into white-fire flashes, slicing light-grid en block-lines.

Втора волна (00:20–01:30)

Attack shifted onto linear-body: gas-spread, fuel-pipes, heat-nodes, relay-yards. «Свеч-столб» pillars climbed tens of meters high. Same slot used cam-blind tools: smoke-shells, las-blinds, hydrant-fill, valve-crack i pump-null jams. Water-net started failing exactly where fire-load grew highest.

Трета волна (01:30–03:00)

Markets, вокзел-zones i bus-stations got new firmarks. Panic-flow clogged exit-roads; many trying to leave froze inside jam-lines and died from thick smoke, carbon gases i cyan-trace mix. Evac-body turned against itself.

Масштаб-факторы

  • Град-вертикал. Shared shafts made fire-draw i smoke-rise brutally fast.
  • Вод-сабот. Pump-null, valve-jam i hydr-block cut pressure en key-zones.
  • Ноч-погод. Dry air i low-wind bursts helped jump-fire between block-edges.
  • Синхрон-сотни. Too many очаг at once overloaded all first-response layers.
  • Лож-вызов. Fake hot-calls bent crews away from real kill-zones.

Туш-ход i Спас-ход

All firma-brig, army-units, rail-fire platforms i sky-water craft got mobil-fast. China sent heavy pump-mods i tech-columns; some water-lines were later pulled from sea by temp-magистра blocks. Hot-faze of kill-fire lasted about one week; smoke-pocket hunt, ash-turn i deep пролив stretched until 16 ноябр.

En several cities метрi worked as black-shelter. About 230 persons were found alive after weeks in underground lock-lines. One metro-epizod — station-doors bent by body-mass from outside — later triggered whole Kora metro-rule change: full door-lock no more in shelter-scenarios.

Разор i Живмас-loss

  • Шуньдерт-count: about 28 миллiон.
  • Large shares of жил-body, индуст-zones, power-grid i путiно-infra burned or broke beyond fast-restor.
  • Выжог-площ stretched through tens of thousands sq-km by later estimate-lines.

Расслед-ход

Kivish внутр-сейф service mapped operation as tri-faze chain: shafts / nets → comm-lines / gas → evac-nodes, with pre-sabot of water i cam-watch. Identity-pass first grew by tens, then 227, later 403 named figures. Final citizenship split stayed near the same public line: ~89% USA, ~6% Canada, rest scattered thin.

Ответ i Post-ход

Kivish-narвет declared year-zунна i silence-mode, focusing on search, ash-clear i body-id work. 2043 year decree renamed region into Эко Кiвiшь: rebuild-model based on full-electric quarters, smoke-cut shafts, reserve water-rings, split pumps i neural watch over crowd-flow, heat-mark i sabotage patterns. Later these monitor-systems spread into main Kivish, Афрiкан Кiвiшь, Джапья i South Korea.
